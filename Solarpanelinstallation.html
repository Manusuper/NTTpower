Info about Solar panel installation in Belgium

In Belgium, the rules and regulations for installing solar panels can vary slightly depending on the region (Flanders, Wallonia, or Brussels). Here are some general guidelines: Regarding Permits and Notifications: In most cases, you do not need a building permit to install solar panels on a roof, provided they are integrated into the roof and do not alter the building's structure. Regarding Technical Requirements: The installation must comply with regional technical standards and safety regulations. And after installation, the system must be inspected and certified by a recognized body to ensure it meets all safety and technical standards.
